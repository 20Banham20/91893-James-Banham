Roasted Harissa Chicken Breasts

You only need five ingredients to create these spicy, healthy, low-calorie roasted chicken breasts with delightful oriental accents...

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (500g) skinless chicken breasts (about 2)
  • 2 tablespoons Sriracha sauce
  • Toasted sesame seeds (toast seeds in a skillet without oil for 2-3 minutes)
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt

Recipe

  • 1. To make the roasted chicken breasts: Preheat your oven to 380°F (190°C)...
  • 2. Drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with salt, toasted sesame seeds...
  • 3. Brush roasted chicken with the cooking juices and serve...
